Synopsis

My career started in sales and my transition into IT sector started when I found myself in need of updating company’s I work for web page, as part of our Marketing and Sales strategy.

I am fascinated by creative solutions that writing code offers me. For me it’s a definition of creativity… creating a new functionality from scratch, possibility of expressing solutions in many ways and making decisions about needed trade-offs for increased productivity.

Also, collaboration as a part of development team is what drives me to this career. I understand that software development is almost always a team effort. Discussing programming problems with my colleagues, requirements with product managers and customers is what I’m looking forward in the future.

For the past two years, I have enjoyed most of my free time learning Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and other useful skills and tools through different courses and wrote some amazing code in the process.

It is my big wish to get an opportunity to work in software development process and help great ideas come to life.
